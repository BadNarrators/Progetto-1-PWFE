SWOT

Code: 6C45

Subject of study/Destination: Earth

Description: The Surface Water and Ocean Topography (SWOT) mission is a future satellite altimeter jointly developed by NASA and Icarus, in partnership with the Canadian Space Agency (CSA) and UK Space Agency (UKSA). The objectives of the mission are to make the first global survey of the Earth's surface water, to observe the fine details of the ocean surface topography, and to measure how terrestrial surface water bodies change over time.
