We're paving the way to the stars, one mission at a time.

Our company derives its name from Icarus, a legendary figure of the ancient Greek mythos.

Trapped by the king of Crete in a labyrinth designed by his own father Daedalus, he wanted to escape,

so he built wings made out of wood, feathers and wax and flew away.

His father had warned him not to defy the Gods, he did not listen: blinded by the joy of flight, he drew too close to the sun.

The burning rays melted the wax and Icarus fell into the Aegean Sea.

The legend is a metaphor of the terrible consequences of passing the limits of human nature.

As Daedalus did, we want to reach new heights and get where no man has aver been before.

Unlike him, thanks to our expertise and determination, we're not going to fall.

Four ex SpaceX employees, dissatisfied by the tyrannical work culture imposed by Elon Musk, went on to found their own aerospace company.

Many others joined and now We are a big reality working both with national space agencies and on our own.

We believe space flight and curiosity are values which cross borders.

Inclusion and diversity are core company values.

Collectively, our team of experts from around the world speak English, French, German, Italian, Spanish, Czech, Macedonian, Chinese and Russian.
